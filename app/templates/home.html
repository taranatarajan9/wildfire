Our Mission

As Californians, we have felt the devastating impact of wildfires firsthand. Wanting to aid in the prevention of further tragedy, we created Wildfire Detector using artificial intelligence. Through computer vision, our product is designed to help fire fighters across the West Coast by alerting them of various fire risks.
